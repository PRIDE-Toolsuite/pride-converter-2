This screen suggests other tools that you might find useful prior to submitting your files to PRIDE.

We recommend that your files are checked thoroughly before submission to PRIDE using PRIDE Inspector. PRIDE Inspector is a desktop application to visualize and perform an initial quality assessment on MS experiments. [PMID:22318026]

Once your files are ready for submission, contact the PRIDE team at pride-support@ebi.ac.uk to find out how to submit your files

Are you interested in performing a submission to ProteomeXchange consortium? The ProteomeXchange consortium has been set up to provide a single point of submission of MS proteomics data to the main existing proteomics repositories, and encourage the data exchange between them so that the community may easily benefit. Contact the PRIDE team about how to proceed.
